Our Story

Crafting Culinary Masterpieces Since 2010

Royal Spice was born from a passionate vision to preserve and celebrate the majestic culinary traditions of India. Founded in the heart of New Delhi, we have spent over a decade perfecting the art of authentic Indian cuisine.

Our journey began with a simple promise — to serve every guest with the warmth of Indian hospitality and the grandeur of royal cuisine. From the fragrant kitchens of Lucknow to the spice markets of Kerala, every dish on our menu tells a story.

The Maestro Behind the Magic

"Indian cuisine is not just food — it is an emotion, a memory, a celebration of life itself."

Chef Arjun Kapoor brings over 22 years of culinary excellence to Royal Spice. Trained at the prestigious Institute of Hotel Management and refined at luxury establishments across Mumbai, London, and Dubai, Chef Arjun is the creative force behind our iconic menu.

His philosophy — honoring ancient recipes while embracing modern elegance — is what makes Royal Spice a destination for discerning food lovers across the globe.
